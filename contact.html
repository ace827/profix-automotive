Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept all insurance companies?
Yes! We work directly with all major insurance companies. Just bring your claim information and we'll handle the rest.
Do you offer fleet and dealer services?
Yes! We offer special pricing for fleet operators and dealerships. Contact us to discuss volume pricing.
What paint brand do you use?
We are a certified Sherwin Williams Automotive Finishes partner. This ensures perfect factory color matching and a premium, durable finish.
How long will my repair take?
Repair time depends on the type of service needed. Simple maintenance takes 1–2 hours, collision repairs can take several days. We'll give you a timeline estimate when you drop off your vehicle.
Do you offer multilingual service?
Yes! We serve customers in English, Spanish, and Portuguese. Everyone deserves quality service in the language they're most comfortable with.
Can I get a free estimate?
Absolutely! Call us at (770) 265-7023 or book online to schedule a free estimate. We'll inspect your vehicle and give you a clear, honest quote.
