FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

Do you accept all insurance companies?

Yes! We work directly with all major insurance companies. Just bring your vehicle and your insurance information — we handle all the paperwork and communication with your insurer.

Do I need an appointment?

Walk-ins are welcome! However, booking an appointment online guarantees your time slot and helps us prepare for your specific service needs.

What paint products do you use?

We are a proud Sherwin Williams Automotive Finishes partner. All our paint and refinishing work uses premium Sherwin Williams products with computerized color matching for a perfect factory finish.

Do you offer fleet pricing?

Yes! We offer special fleet and dealer pricing for businesses. Call us at (770) 265-7023 to discuss your fleet needs and get a custom quote.

How long will my repair take?

Repair times vary based on the type and extent of the work needed. Routine maintenance is often same-day. Collision and body repair typically takes 3-7 business days. We'll give you a clear timeline when you drop off.
