Our Story

Built on Trust, Quality & Commitment

Pro Fix Auto Repair & Collision Inc. was founded with one goal: deliver honest, high-quality auto repair and collision services to the Marietta community. We started as a small shop and grew through word of mouth — because we do the job right, the first time.

Today we offer complete auto services — from routine oil changes to full collision repair, insurance claim management, and Sherwin Williams certified paint jobs. Every vehicle is treated like our own.
