Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Excellence

Pro Fix Auto Repair & Collision Inc. was founded with a single mission: to provide Marietta and the surrounding Atlanta community with honest, high-quality auto repair and collision services at fair prices.

Our team of certified professionals brings decades of combined experience to every job — from routine oil changes to complex collision repairs. We believe every customer deserves transparent communication, expert workmanship, and a vehicle that's returned in better condition than it arrived.

We are proud to be a certified Sherwin Williams paint partner, ensuring every finish we apply meets the highest standards of quality and durability.

Whether you've been in an accident, have a check engine light on, or just need your routine maintenance done right — Pro Fix Auto is here for you, Monday through Friday, 8AM to 6PM.
